\section*{Heroic Inspiration Cog}\stepcounter{section}\phantomsection\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Heroic Inspiration Cog}\label{mi:HeroicInspirationCog}
\indent\textit{Wondrous Item, uncommon}\\
This item can only be used by a construct. When you use an action to integrate the cog into your mechanical body, you gain an Heroic Inspiration. Once this property has been used, it can't be used again, and the cog becomes nonmagical.
